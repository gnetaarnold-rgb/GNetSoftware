Vulnerability Research: Intent Pivots

Testing restricted activity escalation on bootloader-locked devices.

Primary: Home Settings

Attempts to open the default launcher selection. If successful, you can swap the Setup Wizard for a custom launcher.

Launch Home Settings
Secondary: Choose Lock

Attempts to force the "Set PIN/Pattern" screen. Setting a new PIN here often overrides the Google Account requirement upon reboot.

Set New Screen Lock
Tertiary: Search/Google App

Bypasses the settings app entirely to launch the Google Search overlay. From here, searching "Settings" often works when direct links fail.

Launch Google Search
